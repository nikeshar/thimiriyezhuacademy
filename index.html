Why Choose Us?

Thimiriyezhu Silambam academy was started in 2021. It was founded by A R Nikesh. A R Nikesh is a Silambam Master who has 15 years of experience in Silambaatam art. Thimiriyezhu academy teaches Silambatam martial art to marginalised students for free. It not only teaches Silambatam but also that paves a way towards equal society. At present, the students who grew from the academy are now emerging as a trainer.

Our Professional Trainer

A R Nikesh

A R Nikesh is the founder of Thimiriyezhu Silambam academy. He is the first master of the academy. He wanted to train and enlighten the marginalized children and youngsters. Nikesh teaches Silambatam martial art not only to encourage traditional folk arts but also to improve one's self confidence and self respect. His main aim is to create a society with equal opportunities and choices to all through an art.
